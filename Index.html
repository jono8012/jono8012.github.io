About Queenstown

Queenstown, New Zealand, is the Southern Hemisphere’s premier four season lake and alpine resort.

The town is built around an inlet called Queenstown Bay on Lake Wakatipu.It has spectacular views of nearby mountains such as The Remarkables, Cecil Peak, Walter Peak and just above the town. Queenstown is reputed as New Zealand’s favourite visitor destination by its stunning scenery, huge range of activities and renowned warm welcome.

In here, You’ll find everything you need to plan your next trip, from its wide range of accommodation options to the endless array of activities and attractions.

Upcoming Events

Queenstown’s calendar is packed with different sort of events and festivals all year round and You will find many great events in Queenstown every season.

16 April SOUTH ISLAND SPRINTCAR CHAMPIONSHIP, SUPER SALOON STAMPEDE(DECIDER) & CLASS CHAMPS

Easter Sunday and it’s Sprintcarmania Day 2 featuring Cromwell speedway's season finale, the 2016-17 South Island Sprintcar Championship. With the 2016-17 Hydraulink War of the Wings done and dusted, the South’s best of the best gladiators once more wheel out their 800 – 900 horsepower ‘winged chariots’ for adrenalin-pumping, wing-to-wing racing that’s Bigger than Ben-Hur.

18-20 April 2017 Queenstown 4BBB Mixed Tournament

The 2017 Queenstown 4BBB Autumn Mixed Tournament is specifically timed just prior to the 2017 Arrowtown Festival (20-25 April) and the 2017 World Masters Games that is being hosted by Auckland City. The tournament is a two day event played over 36 holes – 18 holes on the fabulous and scenic Jacks Point Golf Course, with the final 18 holes played at the 5-star Millbrook Resort.

19-23 April Queenstown Night Noodle Markets

Expect delicious street-style food, exciting entertainment and the hustle and bustle of an authentic Asian market as the Night Noodle Markets light up the Queenstown Primary School grounds from Wednesday 19 April to Sunday 23 April. More than 50,000 Noodlers came to the markets last time they were in Queenstown. This year it’s going to bigger than ever, with more vendors and a great new location.

20-25 April Akarua Arrowtown Autumn Festival

The Akarua Arrowtown Autumn Festival is a celebration of Autumn for its community. It hosts a variety of events aimed at all ages with our most well know event being the Market Day and Parade on Saturday 22nd April 2017.

30 April Comedy Night with Cori Gonzalez-Macuer & Justine Smith

7Days regular and star of ‘What We Do in the Shadows’, Cori Gonzalez-Macuer dissects the people he desperately hopes his daughter doesn’t turn into…from reality TV “stars” to people who post inspirational quotes. There are a lot of annoying people on this Earth!

3-5 May FINZ Annual Conference 2017

The FINZ Annual Conference is the premier learning event for fundraisers and charity and community sector leaders and those doing great things for good causes. And you might be surprised; for no more than the cost of a conference in any of the main centres, you can book your flights and secure your place, along with excellent accommodation (at the Rydges Lakeland Resort) in Queenstown.

9 May Creative Queenstown Gloria's Handbag

From the creators of Meeting Karpovsky and Playing Miss Havisham comes Gloria's Handbag, a delightful, modern magical mystery with music from Mozart's The Magic Flute, a solo play starring Helen Moulder.
