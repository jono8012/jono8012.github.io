Where to Stay

Queenstown, New Zealand has a huge range of accommodation styles to suit every age and stage, style and budget. Many large hotels and resorts are located centrally within walking distance of the town's centre, and if you’re after something away from the hustle and bustle, hotels line the shores of Lake Wakatipu and can also be found in neighbouring Arrowtown. Motels in Queenstown are a great option for families, couples and business travellers looking for a bit of space to spread out in a self-contained environment. The budget conscious traveller can book into backpacker accommodation, and if you’re travelling with a tent or camper, Queenstown has a number of well-equipped and centrally located holiday parks. If you’re looking for a homely feel try one of Queenstown’s bed and breakfasts in surrounding Arrowtown or Glenorchy.

Hotels:

Queenstown Park Boutique Hotel

Designed with the sophisticated traveller in mind Queenstown Park Boutique Hotel is dedicated to creating a premium experience. The personal attention each guest receives provides an introduction to the best the region has to offer. No matter if visitors are here summer, winter, autumn or spring advice on activities is a specialty.

Millbrook Resort

Eichardt’s Private Hotel, a Queenstown icon, provides fine accommodation in seven luxurious hotel suites in its historic building situated on a premium lakefront position at the heart of Queenstown’s café and restaurant precinct, with some of the finest international shopping labels an easy stroll away. The hotel reflects contemporary provincial elegance, its modern features in perfect harmony with the distinctive antiques and luxurious furnishings of this New Zealand icon.

Eichardt's Private Hotel

Millbrook Resort is an award winning 5-star golf and lifestyle resort. Encompassing five tiers of luxurious accommodation, an award-winning day spa, four restaurants, a 27-hole championship golf course (home of the ISPS Handa New Zeland Open), health & fitness centre, various conference venues and real estate. It caters for on-site weddings, corporate retreats and conferences.

Rydges Lakeland Resort Queenstown

Situated on the shores of beautiful Lake Wakatipu, Rydges Lakeland Resort boasts breathtaking views over the lake and surrounding mountains. We are one of Queenstown's largest hotels and just a 5-minute flat walk along the lakefront to the centre of town. As all 165 rooms has either full or partial views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables, you are guaranteed a breathtaking view of the amazing scenery.

Motels:

Autoline Motel

These recently renovated studios, one and two bedroom apartments are self-contained, stylishly appointed and all fitted with the latest appliances for guests who seek a home away from home. Just a 600 metre stroll to town, and located off the main road, boasts some of the most spectacular mountain and lake views Queenstown has to offer.

Hurley's of Queenstown

Hurley's of Queenstown offers spacious, newly refurbished studios, executive suites and two bedroom apartments located just 5 minutes stroll from Queenstown restaurants, shops and lakefront CBD. All rooms offer super king beds, shower and spa bath, balcony and fully equipped kitchen. An onsite sauna provides the ultimate apres ski relaxation, while the gym gives business travellers or active holiday makers a fitness convenience.

Blue Peaks Lodge

Bright, modern lodge accommodation in the heart of Queenstown with a choice of studios, one or two bedroom units. Our clean and welcoming guest rooms include tea and coffee making facilities, ensuite bathroom with shower and daily room service. Many rooms have full kitchen facilities and all have a fridge and microwave.

Alexis Motels and Apartments

Alexis Queenstown offers a variety of beautifully appointed studio units and apartments, all serviced daily. Enjoy amazing views of Lake Wakatipu and the stunning Remarkables mountain range from all our accommodation options. Situated just a short walk from the vibrant city centre, staying at Alexis Queenstown is high quality and value for money accommodation to suit any one and any budget.
